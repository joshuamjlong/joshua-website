Precision Details
Construction

All Joshua garments are produced within the European Union, using exclusively European components. Each piece is sewn by a small team of seamstresses in Cluj-Napoca, Romania, whose expertise ensures precision, durability, and an uncompromising standard of finish.

Soft, sensual Italian mesh with stretch jersey edge binding provides a snug, flexible hold that moves with the body.

Reinforced inner seams are finished with stretch silk, creating a virtually unbreakable construction that feels soft against the skin.

Straps are designed with a soft, non-slip grip for comfort and security, while custom-made Italian metal components add both refinement and strength.

Crotches lined with organic cotton gussets for breathability, with exterior jersey for a clean finish, and the highest-grade Swiss snaps ensure easy fastening and a secure fit.
