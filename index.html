Construction

All Joshua garments are produced within the European Union, using exclusively European components. Each piece is sewn by a small team of seamstresses in Cluj-Napoca, Romania, whose expertise ensures precision, durability, and an uncompromising standard of finish.

  • Soft, sensual Italian mesh with stretch jersey edging provides a snug, flexible hold that moves with the body.
  • Inner seams lined with stretch silk create an almost unbreakable binding that feels soft against the skin.
  • Straps designed with a soft, non-slip grip for comfort and security, while custom-forged Italian metal components add both refinement and strength.
  • Crotches lined with organic cotton gussets for breathability, with exterior jersey for a clean finish.
  • Precision Swiss snaps ensure easy fastening and a secure fit.

Sizing

All Joshua pieces are crafted from Italian mesh and stretch jersey — materials that conform naturally to the body, moving with you rather than against you. Fit is forgiving across a range of measurements, and most pieces will adapt comfortably to the body within a size.

For bodysuits, we recommend paying particular attention to height. The vertical seams are reinforced with stretch silk, which provides structure and durability but offers less vertical give than the mesh itself. If you are between sizes in height, sizing down will ensure the most comfortable fit through the torso.
