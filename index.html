Construction

All Joshua garments are produced within the European Union, using exclusively European components. Each piece is sewn by a small team of seamstresses in Cluj-Napoca, Romania, whose expertise ensures precision, durability, and an uncompromising standard of finish.

  • Soft, sensual Italian mesh with stretch jersey edging provides a snug, flexible hold that moves with the body.
  • Inner seams lined with stretch silk create an almost unbreakable binding that feels soft against the skin.
  • Straps designed with a soft, non-slip grip for comfort and security, while custom-forged Italian metal components add both refinement and strength.
  • Crotches lined with organic cotton gussets for breathability, with exterior jersey for a clean finish.
  • Precision Swiss snaps ensure easy fastening and a secure fit.

Sizing

All Joshua mesh pieces are crafted from stretch mesh with stretch jersey edging, materials that move with the body rather than against it. Fit is forgiving across a wide range of measurements, and most pieces will adapt comfortably within that range.

For bodysuits, we recommend paying particular attention to height. Vertical seams are reinforced with stretch silk, which lends structure and durability but offers less vertical give than the mesh itself.

When in between sizes, we recommend sizing down for the most comfortable fit.

Shipping

Complimentary standard shipping within the EU via DHL. Express shipping and international delivery options are available at checkout. Orders are dispatched within 1–2 business days.

Returns

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, in their original condition, and in original packaging with all tags attached.

Due to the intimate nature of our products, items showing any signs of wear will not be accepted for return on hygiene grounds. This does not affect your statutory rights.

To initiate a return, contact us at before sending anything back. Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item is faulty or incorrectly sent.

Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5–10 business days of receiving the returned item.
