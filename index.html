Joshua didn't begin as a brand. It began as a response.

Lingerie didn't feel honest. Everything was trying to create a fantasy for my desire or speak over her true personality. Nothing was quiet. Nothing let her decide what it meant.

I made Joshua for women who dress with no one in mind. Not to define her, but to offer a layer she could make her own. Something that sits between lingerie and activewear, between the bedroom and the street.

Joshua is designed for the way she moves, thinks, leaves, returns. For the tension between softness and structure. For the body as it really is: present, private, fluid.

Not about exposure, but presence. Not performative, but always felt.

A first layer that says everything, quietly.

Precision Details
Construction

All Joshua garments are produced within the European Union, using exclusively European components. Each piece is sewn by a small team of seamstresses in Cluj-Napoca, Romania, whose expertise ensures precision, durability, and an uncompromising standard of finish.

  • Soft, sensual Italian mesh with stretch jersey edge binding provides a snug, flexible hold that moves with the body.
  • Reinforced inner seams are finished with stretch silk, creating a virtually unbreakable construction that feels soft against the skin.
  • Straps designed with a soft, non-slip grip for comfort and security, while custom-made Italian metal components add both refinement and strength.
  • Crotches lined with organic cotton gussets for breathability, with exterior jersey for a clean finish.
  • Precision Swiss snaps ensure easy fastening and a secure fit.
